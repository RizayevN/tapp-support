Tapp Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

The short version

Tapp does not collect, transmit, or share any data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What we store

Transactions, budgets, and settings you create are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). This data never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Tapp has:

  • No user accounts
  • No servers
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising
  • No trackers
  • No third-party SDKs

We have no way to access, view, or recover your data.

Data export and deletion

  • Export: Settings → Export CSV gives you a complete copy of your data.
  • Deletion: Settings → Erase all data, or simply delete the app. Both permanently remove all data.

Children's privacy

Tapp collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of Tapp ever changes how data is handled, this policy and the App Store listing will be updated before that version ships.
